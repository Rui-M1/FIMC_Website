The Festival

In development...

This festival was created by Pedro do Carmo, Leonie Asamoah, and Beatriz Lousan, after noticing the lack of opportunities for young musicians to be able to play chamber music and simillars outside of big orchestras.
And specially when covid hit, all the few offers that existed then disappeared.
So the festival became a necessity.
